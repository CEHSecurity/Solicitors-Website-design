Welcome to Solicitors

solicitors is one of the leading firms in the North East specialising in criminal law, divorce, family and child care law.

Established in 1986 we provide the highest quality legal advice and representation in all criminal defence services and all family proceedings and have a long standing reputation in the Teesside area.

Our offices are centrally located in Stockton On Tees and our highly experienced partners are supported by an in house Barrister, associate solicitors and accredited Police station representatives.

All criminal solicitors are members of the Duty Solicitor scheme and our Family solicitors are Children Panel and Family Law panel accredited.
